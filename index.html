Opioid Epidemic's Three Waves

A surge of deaths involving prescription narcotics started in the 1990s and has been followed by spikes involving heroin and illicit fentanyl. Overall opioid fatalities have recently risen to new heights.

Waves
  1. Rise in  prescription opioid  overdose deaths started in 1999
  2. Rise in  heroin  overdose deaths started in 2010
  3. Rise in  synthetic opioid  overdose deaths started in 2013
